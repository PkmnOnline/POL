Most regions feature a dedicated Television Studio, wherein players can participate in various related features.
	Kanto - Lavender Town (Studio Tower)
	Johto - Goldenrod City (Studio Tower)
	Hoenn - Mauville City (TVH - Hoenn TV)
	Sinnoh - Jubilife City (Jubilife TV)
	Orange - Mandarin Island North (Big Orange Broadcast Studios)
	Orre - ?
	Unova - ?
	Kalos - ?
Players can watch various shows on their Pokédexes (if they have reception), or on televisions in secret bases or homes. Some locations have fixed-channel displays, typically running Channel 1 or Channel 2.
