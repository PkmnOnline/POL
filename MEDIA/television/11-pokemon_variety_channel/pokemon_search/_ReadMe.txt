Pokémon Search
	A research team searches for either the red Gyarados, Lugia, Ho-Oh, Deoxys, Rayquaza, or some ghosts in Old Chateau. This is the TV show from the beginning of Diamond and Pearl. Also featured are Pokémon such as a four-winged Zubat attacking workers, and a 100-foot-tall Bellsprout.
