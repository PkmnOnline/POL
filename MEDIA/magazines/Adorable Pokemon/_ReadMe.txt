A magazine published in Sinnoh and Hoenn Pokémon Centers. Gives good tips on pampering your adorable Pokémon. Focuses on Pokémon care and trendy fashions.

(Slated for 32 issues in POL.)

1) Covers practical methods of making your Pokémon happy and gauging Pokémon happiness. Also covers how to avoid making your Pokémon unhappy.

2) Covers the latest fashions and styles and how they project the trainer's image. Includes what kinds of accessories convey SMARTS, TOUGHNESS, BEAUTY, CUTENESS, and COOLNESS.
