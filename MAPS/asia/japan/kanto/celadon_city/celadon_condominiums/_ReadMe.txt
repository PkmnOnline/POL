Celadon Condominiums
	A four-story complex with a front and rear entrance. The roof plaza can only be accessed by the building's rear entrance. Hidden graffiti on the right of the third-floor staircase from the rear entrance depicts "There's graffiti on the wall... Someone added a moustache!".
	The first 3 floors are accessible by stair or elevator. The fourth floor is accessible by elevator only, and even then only with the Lift Key. The Rocket Hideout is also accessible by the elevator here, albeit also only with the Lift Key.
